Smart QR stickers that help lost school items find their way home.

GulfTag AE partners with schools to make lost-and-found a thing of the past. With simple QR stickers on student belongings, staff can scan and instantly see who an item belongs to and how to return it.

Schools choose a plan, students receive their stickers, and from that moment on, lost items finally have a clear way back home.

The problem: lost items cost time, money, and energy

Every day, students lose items around the school, such as bottles, bags, jackets, books, and more. Staff spend waste time collecting and placing these items in a cluttered lost-and-found pile. Parents buy replacements, schools waste time, and students lose their belongings again and again.

The solution: GulfTag AE makes lost items find their way home

GulfTag AE solves this by giving each student a simple QR sticker once they register and pay that links directly to their identification details. When staff find an item, they scan the code and instantly see the owner’s name, class, and school. No searching, no guessing — just a fast and easy return process.

My Personal Experience

A few months ago, I lost my water bottle after PE. When I went to the lost-and-found, it was overflowing with jackets, lunch boxes, and bottles stacked on top of each other.

Mine wasn’t there, and someone had probably taken it because everything looked the same and nobody checks ownership. I remember feeling annoyed and helpless, and I wondered how my parents would react.

Then I realized this happens to students every single day. That is why I am developing GulfTag AE — to make sure items never remain unidentified and can always be returned to their owner.
