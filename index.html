About the Founder

Hi, I’m Madhav, the student behind GulfTag AE. I created this project because I noticed how often students lose their belongings at school and how difficult it is to find the owner. I enjoy coding and building real solutions to everyday problems, and I wanted to make something that actually helps students, parents, and teachers.

CEO Superpower: I am good at coding and use my skills to come up with innovative solutions to daily nuisances.

My Personal Experience

A few months ago, I lost my water bottle after PE. When I went to the lost‑and‑found, it was overflowing with jackets, lunch boxes, and bottles stacked on top of each other. Mine wasn’t there and someone had probably taken it because everything looked the same and nobody checks ownership. I remember feeling annoyed and helpless, and wondered how my parents would react. Then I realized, this happens to students every single day. That is why I am developing GulfTag AE to prevent anything from remaining unidentified.
