How GulfTag works

  1. Parent buys a GulfTag and fills in student details.
  2. We generate a unique GulfTag ID and store it securely.
  3. We print the ID on a sticker and attach it to the student’s belongings.
  4. If found, someone goes to https://gulftag-5c4e6.web.app and enters the ID.
  5. They see the parent’s contact and can call immediately.
