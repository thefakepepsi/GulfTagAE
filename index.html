Welcome to GulfTag AE

Smart QR stickers that help lost items find their way home.

How It Works

1. Tag It — Stick a GulfTag QR on your item.

2. Scan It — Register your item in seconds.

3. Ping It — If someone finds it, they scan and return it.

The Problem

Kids lose bottles, jackets, lunch boxes and bags every day in UAE schools.

Lost-and-found boxes are always full, and parents waste money replacing items.

The Solution

GulfTag AE uses QR stickers to help return lost items quickly and safely.

Scan → See owner → Contact parent → Return item.
