Smart QR stickers that help lost school items find their way home.

GulfTag AE partners with schools to make lost‑and‑found a thing of the past. With simple QR stickers on student belongings, staff can scan and instantly see who an item belongs to and how to return it.

Schools choose a plan, students receive their stickers, and from that moment on, lost items finally have a clear way back home.

The problem: lost items cost time, money, and energy

Every school deals with the same daily challenge: students lose things. Water bottles, lunch boxes, jackets, and pencil cases get left behind in classrooms, buses, and playgrounds. Multiply this by hundreds of students, and the school ends up with a mountain of unclaimed items.

The solution: GulfTag AE makes lost items find their way home

GulfTag AE uses smart QR stickers to connect lost items back to their owners. Staff scan the code and instantly see who it belongs to.

How It Works

1. Find It

Staff find an item with a GulfTag sticker.

2. Scan It

They scan the QR code.

3. Identify

The system shows who the item belongs to.
